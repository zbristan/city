DOUALA

Douala is the chief metropolis of cameroon. It has a population of over 5 million people.
The seaport and airport makes Douala the main entry and exit town for people and goods.

As the regional and Divisional headquaters, The Governor of the Littoral and the SDO of Wouri reside here.

BAMENDA

Bamenda is a town located in the northwestern of Cameroon. It is situated in the volcanic Bamenda highland.Although communication is difficult because of heavy precipitation and rugged relief, the town serves as a trade and export center for local agricultural product such as hides, coffee and tobacco.
